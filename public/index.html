DESCRIPTION
Code Silicon Valley is a student run non-profit education and community service organization founded by current Homestead and Los Altos High students, Sahil Morchi and Siddharth Iyer.
OUR MISSION
Coding is not particularly easy to learn, but that's exactly why it is so valuable. Our goal is to empower the younger generation to learn how to code, especially as computers and technology are becoming a bigger part of our lives day by day. Our classes teach fundamental programming languages for youth and even website design skills.
